BUSINESS DETAILS

About Us:


Founded in 2013, Juana Technologies Pvt. Ltd. grew as a reliable services organization providing consultancy and training services in K12 & Higher Education. We provide Training, Consultancy, Education Solutions (Hardware & Software) and develop, supply and deliver 21st Century Classrooms on Microsoft, SMART Technologies and The Learning Partnership (UK) on new-age Education Solutions for Schools, Colleges & Universities on a range of STEM Curiculum & Competitions to support STEM engagement for students in primary, secondary, higher secondary and college students.
Our Program for Franchise partners in India is a collaboration with The Learning Partnership (UK) and the launch (as first time in India) of our program called "India Innovate: Wheels to Fly!". This program includes Dendrite Platform used by 0.5 million students & teachers in UK, Hong-Kong, South China, UAE and now in India. The program includes a STEM Platform which school teachers and students/parents will use and Two (2) Competitive Programs called "Race to the Line" and "Fly to the Line". Pupils of age group 8-13 will compete as teams of 4 with kits which are provided as part of of the subscription fee. Competitions will be conducted at regional HUB schools and qualifying teams will enter a National Competition which will be held in Delhi. Prizes will be awarded to winning teams. Sponsoring Industry partners will participate in the Regional and National competitions.
India Innovate: Wheels to Fly! Program is an integral part of TLP STEM (UK) where students are inspired to become Engineers. Our Business model is the most UNIQUE and INNOVATIVE business opportunity available in India today. Our students learn designing of products like high speed cars and airplanes using engineering concepts and STEM curriculum available on the Dendrite Platfrom designed by educationist with 15+ years of experience in UK.
