About Us:


At Apollo Health and Lifestyle Limited, India’s first name in corporate healthcare, ‘good health for all’ is the abiding focus. Recognising the need for high quality diagnostics to nip developing health problems in the bud, Apollo offers a wide spectrum of tests through its hospital and clinic facilities across the Country. To expand the offering and increase its accessibility to a wider network of patients and doctors, Apollo Hospitals had launched…
Apollo Diagnostics, a service fully dedicated to providing diagnostics for all age groups. The diagnostic services provide Apollo’s healthcare practitioners with information about the presence,severity and cause of diseases in patients
At Apollo Diagnostics, we are offering Partnering Opportunity to Individuals who have Present/Past exposure in Healthcare industry and wish to establish Apollo Diagnostics - Patient Care Centers. Low cost model and unparalleled returns.
Apollo Group has always been proactive in offering healthcare to meet the changing patterns of diseases and constantly strives to make quality medical care accessible to all. Apollo Diagnostics is a venture undertaken by AHLL to achieve this objective and take the brand presence all over India as well as across international market.
Apollo Health and Lifestyle Limited (AHLL), a wholly owned subsidiary of the Apollo Hospitals Group, headquartered in Hyderabad. It was founded in 2002 with an aim to bring healthcare of international standards within the reach of every individual.
