About Us:


Stellar, a unit of Hi-Life Traders Pvt. Ltd, is a pioneer in the elite furniture and furnishing category in eastern India. Stellar stands for its range of elegant and fine products with not only a diverse collection, but at the same time affirms the quality of all its products

Stellar incepted in the year 1996 with its first showroom at 234/3A, A J C Boss Road, has since its foundation, earned its position as one of the premier furnishings company in Kolkata, which has always stood out in the market due to its noticeable quality and finesse in designs.
