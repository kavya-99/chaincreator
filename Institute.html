BUSINESS DETAILS

About Us:


  • International Training Academy (ITA), a dedicated wing of Institute of International Teachers Training (IITT), is registered under Govt. of India and a leading name in the field of Teacher Training. It is also an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization. ITA`s Educational programmes follow global methodologies & procedures of teaching and training. With the focus on producing future teachers with distinctive set of skills and talents to teach young learners from diverse backgrounds, ITA strongly believes in delivering more value for money.
  • We provide internationally recognized Pre & Primary Teachers Training Program. As a leading teacher training institute, headquartered in Kolkata, ITA (IITT) has gained international acknowledgement by providing courses with all inclusive curriculum, skilled faculty and passionate support team.
  • We provide International (Diploma/ Advanced Diploma/ Post Graduate Diploma and a range of certificate & modular courses including Special Needs Education). These courses ensure thorough learning experiences for the trainees and they cover all the features of childhood education. ITA’s teacher training courses strengthen the teachers’ productiveness by giving them proper knowledge in all areas of early childhood development. Apart from Teacher`s Training courses we also provide Workshops for teachers in various Schools & Workshops for Mothers / Corporate Women who want to guide their children methodically, beyond school.

Our objective:

We at Institute of International Teachers Training are focussed at:
  • Providing our students with an ultimate learning platform for both pre and primary teaching
  • Following the modern procedures and approaches in the field of teaching
  • Producing teachers having distinct set of talents, which help them in understanding the learning ability of the children belonging to the different backgrounds
  • Providing best training practices by the experienced professionals
  • Providing maximum practical experiences to the trainees to enhance their learning and development
