About Us:


Frontier Biscuits - For 94 years we've handcrafted eggless premium biscuits with love. Since our humble beginning in 1921, in Hoti Mardan (now in West Pakistan), we've seen the country fight for freedom, achieve it and witnessed it transform into an emerging global economic superpower.
We too changed with the time. But what remains unchanged for almost a century is our passion for making biscuits in the most delightful way we know - with love and affection. Nutritious and crispy, our products have found a home in many hearts across the whole country.
Every single biscuit is handmade and crafted to perfection to leave every tongue asking for more. And with a huge patronage already relishing our biscuits, through our 60 exclusive Frontier Shopping Zones, we are looking to spread our flavours of goodness even further. So, partner with us and take a bite out of our tasteful legacy.
